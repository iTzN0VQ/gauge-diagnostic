Gauge Diagnostic

Enter your pressures and line temps. The tool computes superheat, subcool, and saturation temps, then runs the NovaCTRL diagnostic engine for a verdict with causes and fixes.

How it works. The tool maps your suction and discharge pressures through a Pressure-Temperature (PT) chart for the chosen refrigerant to compute saturation temperatures. Superheat = suction line temp − evap saturation. Subcool = condenser saturation − liquid line temp. Those numbers (plus your raw pressures) feed the NovaCTRL diagnostic ruleset to flag undercharge, overcharge, restrictions, compressor weakness, and floodback risk — with field-tested causes and fixes for each.
Source. This tool is open-source on GitHub at github.com/iTzN0VQ/gauge-diagnostic — MIT licensed. Fork it, embed it, contribute fixes.
